Hire Us

Step 1

Go to the contact section below and reach out regarding what you are interested in having built for you. Provide a general explanation for the website that you would like built and the features that you would like for it to include. If you would like for us to provide additional services (Ex: product photography, inventory updating, sales management, product delivery service, and social media management) please let us know.

Step 2

We will then contact you in regards to further understand and clarify the specifics of what you would like done. From there you will receive an explanation and general skeleton of what we will be building for you with a pricing quote. You will not be committed to any payment until you approve the initial skeleton and project breakdown. Once the skeleton is approved you will be responsible for covering the quote provided.

Step 3

Once the provided quote has been paid, we will begin to turn your envisioned website into a reality. You will be updated throughout the full building process. At each step we will go over what we are planning to do, verify that it is satisfactory to you, and then proceed with getting it done. This will allow for transparency on our end as well as the ability to have a more hands on approach to make sure the project is custom built to your needs and preferences.
